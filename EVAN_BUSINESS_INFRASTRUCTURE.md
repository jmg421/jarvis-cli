### **What Clerky Provides:**
- **Delaware C-Corp formation** (industry standard)
- **Cap table management** (track equity ownership)
- **Legal document templates** (investment docs, employee contracts)
- **Compliance tracking** (board resolutions, filings)
- **Investor management** (professional documentation)

### **Why Delaware C-Corp:**
- **Investor preference** (all serious investors expect Delaware)
- **Legal framework** (well-established business law)
- **Future fundraising** (required for VC/angel investment)
- **Exit opportunities** (acquisitions expect Delaware structure)
- **Professional credibility** (signals serious business)

### **Clerky Process (7-10 days):**
```
Day 1: Start Clerky account, choose business name
Day 2-3: Complete incorporation paperwork  
Day 4-7: Delaware filing and processing
Day 8-10: Receive corporate documents and EIN
```
